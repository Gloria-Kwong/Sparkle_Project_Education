Past Productions

Fairy Tale "Pinocchio"


2018/2019 Season
Written by Carlo Coollodi

The woodworker Geppetto made a puppet and named him Pinocchio, Pinocchio came to life after Geppetto made a wish for a falling star. However, Pinocchio is so naughty that does not like school. Now he is running away for fun again and his Dad is looking for Pinocchio.

Picture Drama “You Are Special”


2019/2020 Season
Written by Max Lucado

In a small town, all the wooden people named Wemmicks do the everyday task: stick gold stars on those who are beautiful and with smooth wood and fine paint, while stick gray dots on those who look ugly. Punchinello is one of those who receives mostly all gray dots.

Original Children’s Play “Floral Gardener”


2020/2021 Season
Written by Sheila Hu

In the garden of the Queen, there are four beautiful flower fairies: Mimosa, Rose, Sunflower, and Peony. One day, the fairies are informed that the Queen and Princess will visit the garden and select one of the fairies become the Head Gardener. The fairies try to please the Queen and finish the task by all means, and who will be the one?

Historical Drama “The Story of Christmas”


2021/2022 Season
Adapted by Lu Chan

An angel named Gabriel recently receives a new task given by God. The angel is going to inform the virgin, Mary, who is going to be pregnant, the child in the belly is the son of God, name Jesus. With the birth of Baby Jesus, God is with them to save the people who are suffering from the world.
